Fog Computing and Its Applications

The advancement of the Internet of Things has revolutionized the world we live in today. With this expansion, there comes a need for methods that can handle the vast amount of data being generated by the IoT. Fog computing is one such paradigm. To demonstrate the superiority of fog-computing as compared to cloud-based infrastructures, an accident detection application was developed to compare the difference in the response time of fog and cloud.
